# Blu-H ASBL — Statutes (Articles of Association)

This folder contains the **foundational legal documents** governing **Blu-H ASBL**, the non-profit association registered in Luxembourg.

## Legal Status

Blu-H is constituted as an **Association Sans But Lucratif (ASBL)** under Luxembourg law.

- **RCS number:** F15177  
- **RESA publication reference:** RESA_2025_202.553  
- **Publication date:** 17 September 2025  

The statutes were filed and published in the **Recueil Électronique des Sociétés et Associations (RESA)** and are legally effective from the date of publication.

## Authoritative Version

⚠️ **The original French version of the statutes, as published in RESA, is the only legally binding text.**

Any English-language versions provided here are **informational translations only**, published to support transparency, international collaboration, and understanding by non-French-speaking partners, donors, and stakeholders.

In case of any discrepancy, the French RESA version prevails.

## Contents of This Folder

## Amendments and Updates

Any amendment to the statutes must:
1. Be approved by the **General Assembly** in accordance with Luxembourg law;
2. Be filed and published in RESA;
3. Be reflected in this repository with clear versioning and references.

Until such publication occurs, previously published statutes remain in force.

## Transparency and Governance

Blu-H publishes its governance documents openly in line with:
- Good governance practices for non-profit organisations;
- Expectations of international research, humanitarian, and public-interest partners;
- Principles of transparency, accountability, and reproducibility.

For related governance materials (board structure, policies, advisory bodies), see the parent **Governance** directory.
