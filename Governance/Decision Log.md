# Decision Log

This log records **key governance and organisational decisions** taken by Blu-H ASBL.
It is intended to provide continuity, accountability, and institutional memory.

This log does not replace formal board minutes and does not record operational detail.

---

## Decision 008 – Publish Data Protection & Privacy Policy and prepare member register  

**Date:** December 2025  

**Decision:**  
Blu-H decided to publish the Data Protection & Privacy Policy (Public Version) on GitHub immediately, while the website update and official legal document store are still being implemented. Blu-H also decided to begin preparing a members database / member register capability as required by the statutes.  

**Rationale:**  
To support transparency and version control now, while putting the necessary internal record-keeping foundations in place before formal adoption and operational use.  

Decision level: Founders

## Decision 007 – Prepare for Bank Account Opening

**Date:** December 2025  
**Decision:**  
Blu-H decided to prepare for the opening a bank account, creating governance foundations, controls, and

## Decision 006 – GitHub Ownership and Access Controls

**Date:** December 2025  
**Decision:**  
Blu-H confirmed that the GitHub organisation is jointly administered by **three founders
as Owners** to reduce single-person risk and support continuity.

**Rationale:**  
To align with good governance practice, ensure resilience, and support future compliance
with banking, donor, and partner expectations.

**Decision level:** Founders

## Decision 005 – Adoption of Data, Privacy & Ethics Policy (Draft)

## Decision 003 – Creation of Governance Placeholders

**Date:** December 2025  
**Decision:**  
Blu-H created placeholder documents for core governance instruments, including:
- Legal Board Charter
- Delegation of Authority
- Conflict of Interest Policy
- Advisory Board Terms

**Rationale:**  
To signal governance intent and structure while avoiding premature commitments before
board roles, financial controls, and operational scale are finalised.

**Decision level:** Founders 

---

## Decision 002 – Adoption of “Legal Board” Terminology

**Date:** December 2025  
**Decision:**  
Blu-H adopted the term **“Legal Board”** to refer to the statutory governing body
(Conseil d’administration), clearly distinguishing it from advisory roles.

**Rationale:**  
To avoid ambiguity between fiduciary authority and non-fiduciary advisory functions, and
to align with Luxembourg, EU, and humanitarian governance expectations.

**Decision level:** Founders 

---

## Decision 001 – Establishment of Governance Repository

**Date:** December 2025  
**Decision:**  
Blu-H established a dedicated public governance repository on GitHub to act as the
canonical source of governance, policy, and oversight documentation.

**Rationale:**  
To support transparency, version control, and public trust while maintaining appropriate
safeguards for sensitive information.

**Decision level:** Founders
