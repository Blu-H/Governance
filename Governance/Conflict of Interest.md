## Purpose

This policy will define how Blu-H identifies, discloses, and manages actual or potential
conflicts of interest.

The objective is to ensure that all decisions are made solely in the best interests of
Blu-H and its public-interest mission.

---

## Scope

This policy will apply to:

- Members of the **Legal Board**
- Members of the Advisory Board
- Officers, management, and volunteers
- Individuals acting on behalf of Blu-H

---

## Context

Blu-H engages with scientific, humanitarian, academic, and private-sector partners.
Professional, financial, or advisory relationships may give rise to potential conflicts
of interest.

A formal Conflict of Interest Policy will be developed once governance roles and
operational activities are further defined.

## Next Steps

This policy will be drafted and approved by the Legal Board before:

- Receiving external funding
- Entering into formal partnerships
