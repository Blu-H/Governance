## Purpose

This document will define the role and terms of reference for the **Advisory Board** of
Blu-H ASBL.

The Advisory Board provides independent scientific, technical, and strategic advice in
support of Blu-H’s mission.

---

## Nature of the Advisory Board

The Advisory Board is:

- **Non-fiduciary**
- **Non-executive**
- Advisory in nature only

Advisory Board members do not:

- Exercise legal or financial authority
- Act on behalf of Blu-H
- Bind Blu-H to obligations

---

## Context

Blu-H works with advisors drawn from science, technology, business, humanitarian response, and
policy domains.

Formal Advisory Board terms will be developed once:

- The Legal Board is fully established
- Advisory roles and expectations are agreed
- Alignment with individual advisory agreements is confirmed
