# Blu-H Governance

This repository contains the governance framework for **Blu-H ASBL**, a non-profit association registered in Luxembourg.

It documents how Blu-H is governed, how decisions are made, and the principles under which the organisation operates.  
The repository is intentionally public to support transparency, accountability, and institutional continuity.

---

## Purpose of This Repository

The purpose of this repository is to:

- Define Blu-H’s governance structure and principles
- Document roles and responsibilities
- Record significant governance decisions
- Provide an auditable trail for donors, partners, and regulators
- Preserve institutional memory beyond individual roles

This repository represents the **authoritative reference** for Blu-H governance.

---

## Governance Structure (Summary)

Blu-H operates with a clear separation of responsibilities:

### Board of Directors (Conseil d’administration)
- Statutory governing body under Luxembourg law
- Holds fiduciary responsibility for the organisation
- Approves strategy, budgets, and major commitments
- Ensures legal and ethical compliance

### Advisory Boards
- Non-fiduciary and non-executive
- Provide scientific, technical, and strategic advice
- Do not hold decision-making authority
- Typically serve on a pro bono basis

### Operational Management
- Responsible for execution of approved strategy and programmes
- Operates within delegated authority approved by the Board
- Accountable to the Board of Directors
