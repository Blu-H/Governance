1. Purpose

Blu-H is committed to ensuring that its activities do not cause harm and that people engaged with or affected by its work are treated with dignity, respect, and care.

 

This Safeguarding Policy sets out Blu-H’s principles and approach to preventing harm, exploitation, abuse, or inappropriate conduct in the context of humanitarian, scientific, and public-interest activities, including disaster-related contexts.

 

2. Scope

This policy applies to:

• Members of Blu-H

• Board members and advisors

• Volunteers, citizen helpers, and contributors

• Partners and collaborators acting on behalf of Blu-H

• Individuals and communities affected by disasters or crisis events

 

Blu-H’s work may involve challenging international and humanitarian contexts, including disaster-affected regions and vulnerable populations. Safeguarding principles apply in all such contexts, including remote, digital, and data-driven engagement.

 

3. Safeguarding Principles

Blu-H is guided by the following principles:

• Do no harm

• Respect and dignity

• Protection of vulnerable people

• Proportionality

• Accountability

 

4. Protection from Sexual Exploitation, Abuse and Harassment (PSEAH)

Blu-H has zero tolerance for sexual exploitation and abuse (SEA) and sexual harassment (SH) linked to its work.

 

5. Behaviour Standards (Minimum)

All individuals acting on behalf of Blu-H must:

• Never engage in sexual exploitation or abuse

• Never exchange assistance or access for sex

• Never engage in sexual activity with anyone under 18

• Never abuse position or access

• Report safeguarding concerns promptly

 

6. Citizen Helpers and Volunteers

Blu-H commits to avoiding placing volunteers or citizen helpers in unsafe situations and ensuring participation is voluntary and ethical.

 

7. Disaster-Affected Contexts

Blu-H prioritises safety, avoids publishing harmful information, and does not deploy individuals into disaster zones.

 

8. Reporting Concerns

Concerns can be reported confidentially to info@blu-h.org. No retaliation will occur for good-faith reporting.

 

9. Survivor-Centred Response

Blu-H prioritises safety, confidentiality, dignity, and choice in responses.

 

10. Response and Escalation

Blu-H will assess concerns promptly and cooperate with partners or authorities as required.

 

11. Data and Safeguarding

Safeguarding is linked to responsible data use. See Data, Privacy & Ethics Statement.

 

12. Review

This policy will be reviewed periodically.
