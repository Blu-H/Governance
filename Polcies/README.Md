# Blu-H Policies

This folder contains **public-facing policy documents** for Blu-H ASBL, a Luxembourg
non-profit association operating in the humanitarian and public-interest domain.

These policies set out Blu-H’s principles and expectations for ethical conduct, safety,
and responsible practice, particularly in challenging international and disaster-related
contexts.

---

## Purpose of These Policies

The policies in this folder are intended to:

- Support **do-no-harm** humanitarian practice  
- Protect individuals and communities affected by Blu-H’s work  
- Guide the conduct of members, volunteers, advisors, and partners  
- Demonstrate accountability to partners, donors, and the public  

They are written to be **proportionate**, **context-aware**, and **appropriate to an
early-stage organisation**, and may evolve as Blu-H’s activities grow.

---

## Scope

Unless otherwise stated, these policies apply to:

- Members of Blu-H  
- Board members and advisors  
- Volunteers, citizen helpers, and contributors  
- Partners and collaborators acting on behalf of Blu-H  
- Engagement with disaster-affected individuals or communities

## Status and Approval

- Policies may be published in **Draft** form for transparency and review.  
- Final approval authority rests with the ** Legal Board of Directors**.  
- Draft policies do not imply formal adoption until explicitly approved and dated.  

---

## Canonical Versions

The **canonical versions** of these policies are maintained in this GitHub repository.

Word or PDF versions may be shared externally for review or convenience, but the GitHub
versions remain authoritative.

---

## What Is Not Included

For safety, ethical, and legal reasons, this folder does **not** contain:

- Internal procedures or incident reports  
- Personal data or contact details  
- Sensitive operational or security information
